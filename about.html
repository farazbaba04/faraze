The Visionary

Faraz Nadeem — CEO & Founder

Founded by Faraz Nadeem, Farazé was born out of a profound passion for the art of perfumery and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Disappointed by the lack of true, long-lasting craftsmanship in modern commercial scents, Faraz set out to redefine luxury fragrance in Pakistan.

Every bottle under the Farazé label is a testament to this philosophy. From sourcing the rarest global ingredients to the meticulous blending process, Faraz ensures that each creation meets the uncompromising 'Farazé Standard'. This is not just perfume; this is an experience curated for those who command distinction.
